TOP 5 REASONS TO WATCH THIS MOVIE:

REASON 1:
We could all take a page out of Tink’s book when it comes to self-esteem and positivity. She looks in the mirror and celebrates what she sees: her awesome self!

REASON 2:
If you ever happen to lose your shadow—pesky little things that they are—you can count on this pixie to help get you out of a pickle. Or in this instance, out of a drawer.

REASON 3:
If, for example, she finds herself stuck in said drawer, resourceful Tink will devise an escape using whatever she has around her.
REASON 4:
SHE HAS NO TIME FOR SHENANIGANS. When you’re Peter Pan’s right-hand pixie, tomfoolery is beneath you.

REASON 5:
She may seem tough, but Tink has a soft side, especially when it comes to Peter Pan.
Being tiny has perks. Need to provide directions on a dilapidated old pirate map? A quick ink-dip and a few pirouettes later, and Tink will have your course mapped in no time (preferably not one that leads to The Lost Boy’s hideout).
