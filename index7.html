TOP 5 REASONS TO WATCH THIS MOVIE:

  • The movie, while aimed at children, actually sheds a light on emotions in a way that adults can relate to as well.

  • It is a reminder that emotions become more complicated as you get older.

  • Perhaps the most important reason to watch Inside Out with your children is that it will open an honest dialogue between you and your child about emotions.

  • At the end of it all, it is Sadness who saves the day. It brings to the table a very real discussion about depression in children, especially those who are reaching puberty and starting to feel more complicated emotions.

  • It encourages children to tell their parents when they are feeling sad (or any other “negative” emotion).It introduces emotions other than happiness.
